About Secret Comfort

Heels 2 flats, we have YOUR back!!

Mission Statement

Our company,M.A.T Fashion
is developing a type of heel
that can retract and turn into soft,comfortable flats
to help women who suffer from foot pain
when wearing high heels.

General Information

We originated in Atlanta,Georgia.
The founders are Torianna Broaddus,
Aron Daniel,and Miya Ford.

Our Goals

Our main goal is to provide a solution for
women who wear heels and experience
foot pain.We want to make them affordable
for everyone and we plan to give back to
to the community.
